Here's Why

Communicate. Diversify. Elevate.

Communicate: Kayla conducted a poll through the University Committee as an SGA Senator last fall and found that half of students didn’t know what SGA was.

Diversify: Kayla and JT worked together as President’s Leadership Facilitators to help restructure PLC’s admission process to increase diversity.

Elevate: JT has seen how much passion and love students at OSU have and therefore understands the importance of having easy channels for people to express what they care about.

Our Proposed Solutions

Communicate. Diversify. Elevate.

Diversify

O'Colly

Broaden Student Government coverage in order to increase transparency and understanding. Communicate.

SGA Newsletter

Work within SGA to create a newsletter we can email to the student body containing SGA affairs & decisions. Communicate.

Office Hours

Publish the office hours of all SGA officers and Senators publically in order for students to know who represents them and when they’re available. Communicate..

Minority Groups

In order to increase diversity in SGA, SGA needs to be a welcoming organization that diverse students want to be a part of. By creating consistent communication with minority student groups, attending their meetings, and inviting them to ours, we can work toward a better relationship. Diversify.

Freshman Representative Council

Restructure admission for Freshman Representative Council. FRC has proven to be a direct funnel into SGA. If we restructure the selection process and diverge from traditional criteria, we can create a more diverse FRC and, in time, more diverse SGA. Diversify.

Senate Elections

The window to apply to run for SGA Senate needs to be broadly advertised on campus. This would allow students who are not already directly involved in SGA an opportunity become involved. Diversify.

SGA Presence

Through improved communication, representation, and structuring we want to make SGA’s presence on campus more apparent and important to the student body. Elevate.

Seeking to understand

With the increased communication from platform point one, we hope to receive and react to feedback from the student body, further improving SGA’s structure. Elevate.

Governing Bylaws

Evaluate the current SGA bylaws for practicality and to ensure all branches and programs are upholding them. Elevate.
